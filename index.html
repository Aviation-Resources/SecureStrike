Report a malicious phone number

  • Block the number — use iOS “Silence Unknown Callers” or Android “Block Unknown Numbers,” or carrier tools (AT&T Call Protect, Verizon Call Filter, T‑Mobile Scam Shield).
  • Report to the FTC — ReportFraud.ftc.gov · Do Not Call complaint
  • Report to the FCC — Unwanted Calls/Texts complaint
  • Carrier spam reporting — forward the malicious text to 7726 (“SPAM”) to flag the sender with your carrier.
  • Law enforcement — for threats/harassment/fraud attempts, save logs and screenshots and contact local police.
